Cyprus 2023 Family Trip

I feel fortunate to have visited my dad's home country, Cyprus, where my grandmother and one of his sisters currently live. Located in the Eastern Mediterranean Sea, Cyprus is an island of stunning natural beauty. From its crystal-clear beaches and rugged coastlines to its lush, forested mountains, Cyprus truly has it all. Beyond its breathtaking landscapes, the island holds a special place in my heart because of its deep connection to my dad's history. Here are some pictures from my most recent visit during the summer of 2023.
