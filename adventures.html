End of Summer 2024 Roadtrip

As Summer 2024 came to a close, I set off across the Pacific Northwest with my two lifelong friends, a battered tent named ‘Green Grommet,’ and Henry’s Jeep that carried more memories than miles. We crossed into Montana, dipped into Wyoming and Utah, and let the road decide the rest. It was a snapshot of youth—pure, unscripted, and unforgettable. Cyprus 2023 Family Trip

I feel incredibly grateful to have visited Cyprus—my dad’s home country and the place where my grandmother and one of his sisters still live. Nestled in the eastern Mediterranean, the island is a stunning blend of crystal-clear beaches, rugged coastlines, and forested mountains. But beyond its natural beauty, Cyprus holds deep meaning for me. It’s a piece of my family's story, a connection to where my dad came from. These photos are from my most recent trip in the summer of 2023—a visit I’ll always hold close.

A photo with my Yiayia, sister, and cousin during a lovely outdoor lunch at my dad's sister's house.
